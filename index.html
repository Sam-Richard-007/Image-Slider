Fiery-throated hummingbird
The fiery-throated hummingbird (Panterpe insignis) is a species of hummingbird in the "mountain gems" tribe Lampornithini in subfamily Trochilinae.
Golden-browed chlorophonia

Pale blue flycatcher

Blue jay
